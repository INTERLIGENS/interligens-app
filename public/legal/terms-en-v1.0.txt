INTERLIGENS Trusted Investigator Program ("Program") and are accepted alongside
the INTERLIGENS Non-Disclosure Agreement at onboarding.

1. BETA ACCESS
As part of the Program, INTERLIGENS grants the participant free, personal,
limited, and revocable access to a private investigator workspace and related
tools ("Workspace"). Such access is provided solely within the scope of the
Program.

2. NATURE OF ACCESS — NO IMPLIED OBLIGATION OR COMPENSATION

- create any employment relationship of any kind
- create any contractor, service, or consulting agreement
- imply any obligation to produce work, contribute materials, or participate
  in any investigation
- create any right to compensation, payment, or remuneration of any kind
- constitute a commissioned service or ordered deliverable

Access to the Workspace and its tools is a benefit of the beta program.
It is not a work contract, not a mission, and not a debt of production.

Any paid collaboration, compensated mission, or service relationship between
the participant and INTERLIGENS requires a separate written agreement expressly
setting out the scope, deliverables, and compensation. No such relationship
arises from Program participation alone, regardless of the quantity or quality
of work produced within the Workspace.

Access to the Workspace is:
- Free of charge
- Personal and non-transferable
- Limited to the scope of the Program
- Revocable at any time by INTERLIGENS, with or without cause, with no

5. ACCESS LEVELS
Access within the Program is progressive:
- Beta Investigator: private workspace, cases, tools, private export
- Verified Investigator: same, with higher internal trust designation
- Trusted Contributor: eligible for Intel Vault submissions, public attribution,
  Trusted Contributor badge

Upgrade to Trusted Contributor requires satisfying objective criteria reviewed
and approved by INTERLIGENS.

INTERLIGENS reserves the right to suspend or permanently revoke access in the
event of NDA breach, trust concern, security concern, publication abuse, or

suspension or revocation.
